# Explorer Pipeline Video — Production Plan

## Phase 2: Global Definitions

### Visual Style Specification
- **Sub-genre:** Dark cosmic sci-fi with anime energy bursts
- **Rendering + Line:** Semi-realistic digital painting, glowing edge highlights
- **Color + Lighting:** Deep navy/black backgrounds, gold/amber accents, cyan energy pulses, dramatic rim lighting
- **Detail density:** Moderate backgrounds (star fields, nebulae), high detail on key subjects (clock, doorways, characters)

### Recurring Elements
1. **Civilisation Clock** — Large ornate clock face, Roman numerals, golden hands, approaching midnight, cosmic gears visible
2. **Three Doorways** — Three glowing archways: amber (Spinner), emerald (Dungeon), cyan (Grey Matter)
3. **Young Explorer** — Silhouette of a young person (gender neutral, 12-ish), backlit by cosmic light

### Voice Profile
- **Off-screen narrator:** Female, young (20s), high energy, K-Pop Guide style
- **Tone:** Excited, breathless at big numbers, dramatic pauses, punchy delivery
- **Pace:** Fast-slow-fast (anime pacing), slows for impact moments
- **Language:** English

### BGM
- **Source:** Separate (search royalty-free)
- **Style:** Upbeat electronic/synth, anime opening energy
- **Tempo:** 120-130 BPM
- **Instruments:** Synth pads, driving beat, orchestral swells at climax

## Phase 3: Clip Planning

### Clip 1: THE HOOK (6 seconds)
- **narrative_purpose:** establish
- **pacing:** moderate
- **scene:** Deep space, stars, a massive ornate clock floating in the void, hands at 11:50
- **content_action:** Camera slowly pushes toward the clock as it ticks closer to midnight
- **transition_description:** The scene opens in deep space with distant stars and nebulae. A massive golden Civilisation Clock floats at center frame, its ornate Roman numeral face glowing warmly against the cold void. The clock hands are at 11:50, ticking steadily. The camera slowly dollies forward toward the clock face, the ticking growing louder, the glow intensifying as we approach. Stars streak past the edges of frame.
- **duration:** 6
- **camera_movement:** dolly
- **first_keyframe_framing:** Wide shot, centered, straight-on
- **first_keyframe_visible_content:** Deep space background, massive golden clock at center, hands at 11:50, stars and nebulae
- **last_keyframe_framing:** Medium shot, centered, straight-on, closer to clock
- **last_keyframe_visible_content:** Clock face fills more of frame, hands at 11:51, golden glow intensified, gear mechanisms visible
- **last_keyframe_edit_from_first:** no
- **inter_clip_boundary:** scene_cut
- **first_keyframe_reuse:** no
- **last_keyframe_required:** yes

- **bgm_source:** separate

- **narration_cue:** "What if saving civilisation... was a game?"
