# Youth Explainer Video Scripts — Ages 8-14
## Three Videos, One Per Explorer Sub-Mode

**Voice:** Energetic young female, K-Pop Guide style (per TP-018)
**Tone:** Excited older sister who genuinely thinks this is amazing
**Language:** Simple, punchy, no jargon, no percentages, no formulas
**Duration:** ~20-25 seconds each (4 clips per video)

---

## VIDEO 1: RELAY SPINNER — "Spin the Story of Everything"

### Clip 1 (6s) — THE HOOK
**Narration:** "Everything you use — roads, phones, the internet — somebody had to invent it. And it all started with ONE thing."
**Visual:** Cosmic zoom from space down to Earth, landing on a campfire. Modern city skyline fades in behind it. Stars and golden sparks.

### Clip 2 (6s) — THE GAME
**Narration:** "Twelve inventions changed the world forever. Spin the reels to find them all!"
**Visual:** A glowing arcade slot machine with relay symbols spinning — fire, tree, river, horse. Reels lock in: JACKPOT! Coins and sparks shower everywhere. Anime energy lines.

### Clip 3 (6s) — THE STAKES
**Narration:** "Every discovery you make pushes the clock back from midnight. The world is counting on players like you."
**Visual:** A giant cosmic clock with hands approaching midnight. A kid's hand reaches out and pushes the minute hand backward. Golden light bursts from the clock face. Stars swirl.

### Clip 4 (4s) — THE CTA
**Narration:** "Ready? Grab the lever. Spin!"
**Visual:** Close-up of a glowing golden lever. A hand pulls it down. Reels blur into motion. Screen flashes with "RELAY SPINNER" title in arcade font. Sparkle burst.

**Total narration: ~22 seconds**

## VIDEO 2: DUNGEON CRAWL — "Your Adventure Through 12,000 Years"

### Clip 1 (6s) — THE HOOK
**Narration:** "Imagine exploring a cave where humans first discovered fire. You're standing right there. What do you do?"
**Visual:** First-person view entering a torchlit cave. Ancient handprints and spirals glow on the walls. Warm firelight flickers. Anime-style sparkle particles.

### Clip 2 (6s) — THE GAME
**Narration:** "Every room is a choice. Every choice is a discovery. And DAVID — your guide — is with you the whole way."
**Visual:** A parchment-style dungeon map unfolds showing connected rooms. A glowing character token moves room to room. DAVID appears as a friendly holographic guide figure. Choice buttons glow below.

### Clip 3 (6s) — THE STAKES
**Narration:** "Twelve dungeons. Ninety-one inventions hidden inside. The deeper you go, the more you learn about how we built... everything."
**Visual:** Quick montage: forge chamber with glowing metal, river crossing with ancient boats, a workshop with spinning looms, a launchpad with rockets. Each scene transitions with a golden door opening.

### Clip 4 (4s) — THE CTA
**Narration:** "Roll the dice. Enter the dungeon. Your story starts now."
**Visual:** A glowing d20 dice rolls across screen and lands on 20. A dungeon door swings open with golden light pouring out. "DUNGEON CRAWL" title appears in fantasy font. Sparkle burst.

**Total narration: ~22 seconds**

## VIDEO 3: POWER OF GREY MATTER — "Unlock the Powers That Built Everything"

### Clip 1 (6s) — THE HOOK
**Narration:** "What if every great invention in history could give you a superpower? Fire gives you a shield. Ships give you a compass. Rockets give you wings."
**Visual:** A young anime-style character stands in a cosmic arena. Three power icons orbit around them — a flame shield, a ship's compass, wings made of light. Energy crackles.

### Clip 2 (6s) — THE GAME
**Narration:** "Complete missions across twelve relays. Each one you master gives you a new power. Collect all twelve and something incredible happens."
**Visual:** A circular Power Wheel with 12 segments, each lighting up one by one with different colours — fire red, tree green, river blue. Energy pulses connect them. The wheel spins and glows brighter with each power.

### Clip 3 (6s) — THE TRANSFORMATION
**Narration:** "You transform. From apprentice... to iMan. The full power of twelve thousand years of human invention — in your hands."
**Visual:** The character glows, energy gathers from all 12 power icons, lightning strikes, dramatic transformation sequence — the simple silhouette becomes a fully powered-up glowing figure. "iMAN" text blazes across screen. Cosmic explosion of golden light.

### Clip 4 (4s) — THE CTA
**Narration:** "Thirty-seven minutes a day. That's all it takes. Are you in?"
**Visual:** The Civilisation Clock with hands moving backward away from midnight. The transformed iMan figure stands before it, arms raised. "POWER OF GREY MATTER" title appears. Stars and golden particles.

**Total narration: ~22 seconds**

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**What kids understand (used in scripts):**
- Time ("12,000 years", "midnight", "37 minutes a day")
- Games ("spin", "roll the dice", "collect", "unlock")
- Superpowers ("shield", "compass", "wings", "transform")
- Stakes ("the world is counting on you", "push the clock back")
- Agency ("what do you do?", "your story", "are you in?")

**What kids DON'T understand (avoided):**
- Percentages ("0.000267%")
- Formulas ("S = (A×P)/β")
- Abstract concepts ("fragility multiplier", "dimensionless constant")
- Academic language ("operant conditioning", "variable ratio reinforcement")
- Big numbers without context ("375,000×")

**The Blip translated for kids:**
- Instead of "0.000267%": "If Earth's history was one day, everything humans built happened in the last millisecond before midnight"
- Instead of "Survival Formula": "Every discovery pushes the clock back from midnight"
- Instead of "2,229 seconds": "Thirty-seven minutes a day — that's all it takes"
- Instead of "1 billion learners": "The world is counting on players like you"
- Instead of "Cosmic Counterweight": "You transform — the full power of 12,000 years in your hands"

**BGM:** Same anime-style energetic track as the pipeline video, or search for a new upbeat royalty-free track with arcade/adventure energy.
