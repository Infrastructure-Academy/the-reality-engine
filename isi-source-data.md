## ISI Calculator (from Memorial Site /isi page)
Formula: ISI = (A × P) / β

- P = Panel Size (Number of independent reviewers) — slider 1-50
- β = Bias Correction factor — slider 0.5 (favourable) to 3.0 (extreme), 1.0 = neutral

Methodology: The ISI formula weights accuracy by panel size and corrects for systematic bias.
A larger panel with the same accuracy produces a higher ISI, reflecting greater statistical confidence.
The bias factor β penalises known methodological weaknesses.
