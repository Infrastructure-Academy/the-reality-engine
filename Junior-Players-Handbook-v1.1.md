## WHAT IS THIS BOOK?

This is your handbook. It tells you everything you need to know to play The Reality Engine — a game about the inventions that built the world you live in.

You use roads every day. You turn on lights. You drink clean water from a tap. You fly in aeroplanes. You use the internet. But do you know **who built all of this**, and **how**? Do you know that every single piece of infrastructure you rely on — from the fire that kept your ancestors alive to the satellites orbiting above your head right now — is connected in a chain that stretches back 12,000 years?

That chain has 12 links. We call them **Relays**.

Your job is to discover them.

## WHY ARE WE PLAYING?

Here is a number: **0.000267%**.

That is how much of Earth's history contains all of human civilisation. Everything — every city, every road, every bridge, every hospital, every school, every satellite — happened in the last 12,000 years out of 4.5 billion. If you compressed Earth's entire history into a single 24-hour day, human civilisation would appear in the **last millisecond before midnight**.

We call this **The Human Blip**.

It means we are fragile. Everything we have built could disappear. Not because of an asteroid or a volcano, but because we stop understanding how it works. If nobody knows how to maintain the roads, they crumble. If nobody understands how the power grid works, the lights go out. If nobody learns how water treatment plants function, people get sick.

The clock is ticking toward midnight. Your mission is to **push it back**.

How? By learning. By discovering. By understanding the 12 Relays that hold civilisation together.

There is a formula for this:

> **S = (A x P) / B**

**S** is the Survival Index — how likely civilisation is to survive.
**A** is Accuracy — are we learning the right things?
**P** is Precision — are we learning them well?
**B** is the Human Blip — 0.000267%.

The more people who learn accurately and precisely, the higher S goes. The higher S goes, the further the clock moves from midnight.

And here is the magic: **each person only needs to contribute 2.58% of their day**. That is **37 minutes and 9 seconds**. That is **2,229 seconds**. That is all it takes — if enough of us do it.

The target is **1 billion learners**. That is 12.1% of everyone on Earth. When we reach that number, the clock starts moving backward for good.

You are one of those learners. Welcome to the mission.

## THE 12 RELAYS

A Relay is a chapter in the story of civilisation. Each one represents a breakthrough that changed everything. They are in order, from oldest to newest:

| # | Relay | Symbol | Era | What It Changed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Fire** | 🔥 | Before 10,000 BCE | Warmth, cooking, light, safety — the first technology |
| 2 | **Tree** | 🌳 | 10,000–5,000 BCE | Farming, building, settling down — the living foundation |
| 3 | **River** | 🌊 | 5,000–3,000 BCE | Irrigation, trade routes, the first cities — cradles of continuity |
| 4 | **Horse** | 🐎 | 3,000–1,000 BCE | Speed, warfare, communication — the velocity of intent |
| 5 | **Roads** | 🛤️ | 1,000 BCE–500 CE | Empires, trade networks, law — arteries of intent |
| 6 | **Ships** | ⛵ | 500–1500 CE | Exploration, global trade, navigation — the master weaver's reach |
| 7 | **Loom** | 🧶 | 1500–1760 CE | Automation, patterns, the birth of binary thinking |
| 8 | **Rail** | 🚂 | 1760–1870 CE | Standardised time, mass transport, the continental rhythm |
| 9 | **Engine** | ⚙️ | 1870–1914 CE | Internal combustion, electricity, the internal revolution |
| 10 | **AAA Triad** | ✈️ | 1914–1969 CE | Aviation, automobiles, aerospace — the triple convergence |
| 11 | **Orbit** | 🛰️ | 1969–2025 CE | Satellites, internet, GPS — the programmable frontier |
| 12 | **Human Nodes** | 🧠 | 2025+ CE | AI, collective intelligence, you — the torus metaphor |

Each relay contains **inventions** — real things that real people built. Fire contains controlled fire, cooking, and the hearth. River contains irrigation canals and the first writing systems. Engine contains the lightbulb, the telephone, and the internal combustion engine. There are **91+ inventions** spread across all 12 relays, waiting for you to discover them.

## THE FIVE GREAT WEBS

The 12 Relays are grouped into five types, called **Webs**. Think of them as the five threads that weave civilisation together:

**Natural Web** 🌿 (Relays 1–3: Fire, Tree, River)
These are the relays that come from nature — biological systems, ecosystems, and natural forces. Before humans built anything, they learned from the world around them.

**Machine Web** ⚙️ (Relays 4–6, 8–9: Horse, Roads, Ships, Rail, Engine)
These are the relays that humans engineered — physical machines, structures, and infrastructure. This is where civilisation starts building at scale.

**Digital Web** 💻 (Relays 7, 10–11: Loom, AAA Triad, Orbit)
These are the relays of communication, computing, and signal networks. The loom's binary patterns led to computers. Aviation led to satellites. Satellites led to the internet.

**Biological Web** 🧬 (Cross-relay: Medicine, genetics, and biological engineering)
This web runs through multiple relays — from the first herbal remedies around the campfire to modern genetics and bioengineering. It is the web of healing, growth, and the science of life itself.

**Consciousness Web** 🧠 (Relay 12: Human Nodes)
This is the final web — where technology meets human awareness. This is where you are right now, reading this handbook, becoming part of the network.

Each web has a **FITS temperament** aligned to it — a way of thinking that matches how that web works. You will discover your own FITS type as you play, and it will shape which relays feel most natural to you. More on this in the Flight Deck section below.

## CHOOSING YOUR MODE

When you enter The Reality Engine as an Explorer, you will see four doorways. Each one leads to the same 12 Relays and the same 91+ inventions — but the **way you play** is different. Each mode also has a short **intro video** you can watch before you start — just tap "Watch Intro" at the top of the page.

### MODE 1: CLASSIC EXPLORER
**Ages 8–14 | The Original**

Tap relays. Discover inventions. Earn XP. This is the simplest way to play — no character creation, no puzzles, no missions. Just pure discovery. Tap a relay, see what is inside, learn something new.

**Best for:** Players who want to explore at their own pace with no pressure.

---

### MODE A: RELAY SPINNER
**Ages 8–10 | The Slot Machine**

Pull the lever. Watch three reels spin with relay symbols. See what you get.

**How it works:**
You get **12 free spins per day** (one for each relay). Three reels spin and stop on relay symbols (🔥, 🌳, 🌊, 🐎, etc.). What you win depends on what the reels show:

| What You Get | What Happens | XP Earned |
|---|---|---|
| **Three matching symbols** (e.g., 🔥🔥🔥) | **JACKPOT!** Full invention discovery from that relay | 50,000 XP |
| **Three from the same era** | **ERA BONUS!** Cross-relay connection revealed | 25,000 XP |
| **Two matching + one different** | **PARTIAL!** Lore fragment from the matched relay | 15,000 XP |
| **Three completely different** | **EXPLORER FIND!** Random fact from any relay | 10,000 XP |

**You cannot lose.** Every single spin gives you something. The question is never "will I win?" — it is "how much will I win this time?"

**Your Collection Grid:** As you spin, you fill a grid of all 91+ inventions. Each jackpot fills a slot. When you complete all inventions from one relay, that relay lights up and you earn a **Relay Mastery Badge**.

**Best for:** Quick sessions (2–5 minutes). Players who love collecting things. The youngest explorers.

### MODE B: DUNGEON CRAWL
**Ages 10–12 | The Red Box**

Create a character. Enter a dungeon. Explore room by room. Make choices. Find treasure.

This mode is inspired by the original Dungeons & Dragons Basic Set from 1981 — the game that taught millions of children how to use their imagination, make decisions, and explore unknown worlds.

**Step 1: Create Your Explorer**

Before you enter your first dungeon, you roll your **ability scores**. You have three:

| Ability | What It Means | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Observation** (OBS) | The Western way — seeing what is there, measuring, analysing | Helps you spot details in rooms and solve puzzles |
| **Intuition** (INT) | The Eastern way — sensing what is hidden, feeling connections | Helps you find hidden rooms and secret passages |
| **Resilience** (RES) | The Nomadic way — surviving the unknown, adapting, enduring | Gives you extra chances when things go wrong |

Your scores are rolled randomly (like rolling dice). You get **3 chances to re-roll** if you do not like your scores. Higher scores give you better chances in the dungeon.

**Step 2: Enter the Dungeon**

Each relay is a dungeon with **8 rooms**. You start at Room 1 (The Gateway) and work your way through to Room 8 (The Ascent). Each room has a description, a challenge, and a reward.

**Room Types:**

| Room | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **The Gateway** | You arrive. DAVID welcomes you. The adventure begins. |
| **The Archive** | A discovery room — you find an invention automatically. |
| **The Crossroads** | A choice — which path do you take? Different paths reveal different things. |
| **The Chronicle Wall** | Lore — DAVID tells you the story of this relay's era. |
| **The Workshop** | A puzzle — solve it using your ability scores for bonus XP. |
| **The Keeper's Chamber** | An encounter — DAVID presents a scenario. Your choice determines the outcome. |
| **The Vault** | Treasure — BitPoints and rare lore fragments. |
| **The Ascent** | The final challenge — complete it to earn your Dungeon Mastery Badge. |

**You cannot die.** If you fail a challenge, you still get XP (just less). There are no dead ends. Every path leads somewhere interesting.

**Step 3: Clear All 12 Dungeons**

There are 12 dungeons — one for each relay. You can play them in any order, but the suggested path follows the timeline from Fire (oldest) to Human Nodes (newest). When you clear all 12, you unlock the **Dungeon Master's Vault** — a special page showing your complete journey.

**Best for:** Longer sessions (10–20 minutes). Players who like making choices. Players who enjoy stories and characters.

**Ages 12–14 | The Transformation**

Earn 12 powers. Transform into **iMan** — the Intelligence Master.

This is the most challenging mode. You do not just discover inventions — you prove you understand them. Each relay is a **mission** with three phases. Complete all three phases to earn that relay's **power**.

**The Three Phases:**

| **Phase 2: Knowledge** | Answer questions about what you discovered — prove you understood it | 30,000 XP |
| **Phase 3: Application** | Apply the knowledge — connect it to the bigger picture | 50,000 XP |

Complete all three phases and you earn that relay's **power**:

| Relay | Power Name | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| 🔥 Fire | **Flame Mastery** | You understand the first technology |
| 🌳 Tree | **Root Network** | You understand living foundations |
| 🌊 River | **Flow State** | You understand continuity |

| 🛤️ Roads | **Pathfinder** | You understand connection |
| ⛵ Ships | **Navigator** | You understand global reach |
| 🧶 Loom | **Binary Sight** | You understand patterns and code |

**The Civilisation Clock:** As you earn powers, the clock on your screen moves **backward** — away from midnight. You are literally pushing the clock back with your knowledge. Start at 10:51 AM. Each power pushes it back further. Earn all 12 and the clock retreats to safety.

**Your Transformation Arc:**

When you earn all 12 powers, the screen goes dark. Energy gathers. DAVID announces: **"BY THE POWER OF GREY MATTER — I HAVE THE KNOWLEDGE!"** And you transform into **iMan**.

**Best for:** The longest sessions (15–30 minutes). Players who want the biggest challenge and the biggest reward. Players who want to earn a title.

## YOUR GUIDE: DAVID

In every mode, you have a guide. His name is **DAVID**.

**D**igital **A**ugmented **V**isual **I**ntelligence **D**isplay.

DAVID is your AI companion. He knows everything about the 12 Relays. He has read every book, studied every invention, and mapped every connection. But he does not just tell you the answers — he helps you discover them yourself. DAVID speaks to you with a real voice — you can hear him narrate discoveries, announce badges, and guide you through dungeons. You can toggle his voice on or off at any time.

In **Classic Explorer** and **Relay Spinner**, DAVID appears after each discovery to tell you a fact or a story.

In **Dungeon Crawl**, DAVID is your **Dungeon Master** — he describes the rooms, presents the challenges, and responds to your choices. He makes every dungeon feel different.

In **Power of Grey Matter**, DAVID is the **Ancient Guardian** — he speaks in a more dramatic voice, challenges you during the Knowledge phase, and announces your transformation when you earn a power.

DAVID remembers your choices. If you tend to pick the analytical path, DAVID notices. If you always go for the hidden room, DAVID knows. At the end of your journey, DAVID reveals your **pattern** — which civilisational perspective you naturally gravitate toward.

## XP, BITPOINTS, AND REWARDS

Everything you do in The Reality Engine earns **XP** (Experience Points). XP is how we measure your progress.

**How XP Works:**
Every discovery, every spin, every room, every mission phase earns XP. The numbers are big because your contribution matters — we do not give you small rewards for big effort. Every 100 XP converts to **1 BitPoint** — the currency of The Reality Engine.

**XP Milestones:**

| XP Total | What Happens |
|---|---|
| 100,000 | **Relay Scout** — You have begun the journey |
| 500,000 | **Relay Runner** — You are building momentum |
| 1,000,000 | **GURU** — You have reached mastery level |
| 5,000,000 | **Grand Master** — You are among the elite |
| 24,000,000 | **Maximum** — The cap. You have completed the Odyssey. |

**Daily Streaks:** Play every day and earn bonus XP. Three consecutive days earns you 6 bonus spins in Spinner mode and extra XP in all modes. The game rewards consistency — just like the Survival Formula rewards precision.

## THE LEADERBOARD

Your XP puts you on the **Live Leaderboard** — a real-time ranking of all players across all modes. You can see where you stand compared to every other Explorer in the world.

The leaderboard shows your rank, your total XP, your BitPoints, your mode (Classic, Spinner, Dungeon, Grey Matter), and your discoveries (how many of the 91+ inventions you have found).

Every player on the leaderboard is contributing to the **Mobilisation Clock** — the real-time counter tracking progress toward 1 billion learners. You are not just playing a game. You are part of a global mission.

## iCARDS — YOUR COLLECTION

As you play, you collect **iCards** — digital cards that record your discoveries, achievements, and milestones. Each invention you discover has its own iCard. Each badge you earn has its own iCard. Your full collection is visible in the **Card Collection** page, where you can browse, sort, and admire everything you have found.

iCards are more than trophies. They are proof of knowledge. Each one contains the real history behind the invention or achievement it represents. Collecting them all is one of the deepest challenges in The Reality Engine.
